Sushi

Ingredients

  • Japanese sushi rice
  • Rice wine vinegar
  • Nori
  • Wasabi plate
  • Smoked salon
  • Cucumber
  • Avocado

Recipes

  1. Immediately after rice is cooked, mix in rice wine vinegar; spread rice on a plate until completely cool.
  2. Place 1 sheet of seaweed on a bamboo mat; press a thin layer of cool rice on seaweed, leaving at least a 1/2 inch top and bottom edge of the seaweed uncovered. This is for easier sealing later. Dot some wasabi on rice; arrange smoked salmon, cucumber, and avocado on rice about 1 inch away from the bottom edge of seaweed.
  3. Slightly wet the top edge of seaweed; roll up tightly from the bottom to the top edge with the help of the bamboo mat. Cut salmon roll into 8 equal pieces and serve. Repeat for other rolls.
